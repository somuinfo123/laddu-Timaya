Welcome to Laddu Laddu Timmaya

Our products are made by rural women from North Karnataka, bringing you an authentic taste that is rooted in tradition.

We take pride in ensuring that no preservatives are used in our products. Additionally, we do not use refined sugar or palm oil, ensuring pure and healthy treats for you.

Our laddus and sweets are handcrafted with love using natural and locally sourced ingredients, keeping alive the rich culinary heritage of our region.

By purchasing from us, you are directly supporting rural women artisans, empowering them financially and helping them achieve self-sufficiency. This initiative provides these women with a sustainable source of income, enhances their skills, and promotes their invaluable contribution to preserving traditional recipes.

Experience the richness of homemade goodness and support rural women artisans with every bite!

Health Benefits of Pure Ghee and Jaggery

Pure Ghee: Rich in healthy fats and essential vitamins, pure ghee aids in digestion, boosts immunity, and provides sustained energy. It is also known for its anti-inflammatory properties and benefits for skin and brain health.

Jaggery: A natural sweetener packed with minerals and antioxidants, jaggery helps in detoxifying the body, improving digestion, and boosting iron levels. Unlike refined sugar, it provides energy without harmful side effects.

Our sweets combine these natural ingredients to bring you both taste and health in every bite!
